Cherry Wild

..is made with whole/sliced black, bing and sour cherries with cherry pit oil. The perfect cherry ice cream!

Blue Paradise

...is a blend of Tropical Fruits with Blue Spirulina, this sorbet has a refreshing tropical taste and no artificial ingredients.

Full of berries

...includes elderberry, genus Sambucus, has an awesome flavor when treated well; good tasting, good for you, and great color for your ice cream cone.

Yin and Yang

...is made up of half subtle, spectacular vanilla (made with real vanilla extract) and half really rich, dark, chocolate ice cream.

Choco Hurricane

...is made up of half subtle, spectacular vanilla (made with real vanilla extract) and half really rich, dark, chocolate ice cream twisted together with a pinch of Cinnamon.
